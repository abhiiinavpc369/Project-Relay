## prd.md (Product Requirements Document)

### Goal

Build a secure, fast, and easy‑to‑use chat application with strong privacy guarantees.

### Must‑Have Features (MVP)

* User registration & login
* One‑to‑one chat
* Real‑time messaging
* Message delivery status (sent, delivered, read)
* End‑to‑end encryption
* Online / offline presence

### Nice‑to‑Have (Phase 2)

* Group chats
* Media sharing
* Message reactions
* Voice notes
* Chat backup (encrypted)

### Non‑Goals (For Now)

* Public channels
* Ads
* AI auto‑reply in chats

### Constraints

* Low bandwidth support
* Works on low‑end devices
* Strong encryption without hurting UX
